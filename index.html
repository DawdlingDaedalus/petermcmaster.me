About

Personal Profile

My love of technology began with a love of mathematics. In high school, I remember my sheer amazement at learning how to use calculus to model acceleration and trajectory. In college, I worked heavily in Econometrics with the dream of building the sustainable future of industrial society. Most recently, I've been learning computer science, software engineering, linear algebra, and statistical methods for producing artificial intelligence.


About three years ago, a friend clued me in to the vast upheavals bound for a world of AI. Since then, I have been consuming everything I can find on the subject. I learned to code. And I completed a Machine Learning Engineering Bootcamp involving over 600 hours of project-based learning.


In my spare time, I enjoy outdoor sports such as surfing and hiking. I live in Los Angeles with my girlfriend, Leah, and our two cats. When I'm not coding or studying, I can be found dangling a feather on a string for our furry scoundrels.
